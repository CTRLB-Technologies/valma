Business Challenge

Think and Learn or Byju’s Learning app, being an unicorn in education app sector, outsourced their production work to eligible vendors. The need to contribute to their gaming division with assets that they will integrate for their class 6-10 subjects. The challenge is to meet very tight schedules which they have.

Solution

For Valma Creations, this was an opportunity. It was tough passing their rigorous test and also to meet their highly squeezed timelines. Our team of 5 Bg (color and layout) artist, 3 animators, 1 comp and 1 Co-Ordinator team did a very good job, In achieving and maintaining the demand for very high quality work. We worked on 29 ‘Experiences’ till the time, when Byjus’ were in market crisis. The association went for a year and still on the Vendor list.
